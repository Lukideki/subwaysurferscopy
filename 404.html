Welcome to Subway Surfers!

About the Game:
Subway Surfers is an endless runner mobile game. Run as fast as you can through the subway system while avoiding obstacles!

How to Play:

  • Use LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move sideways
  • Press UP arrow key to jump
  • Press DOWN arrow key to roll
  • Press SPACE to equip/use hoverboard
  • Collect coins and power-ups
  • Avoid obstacles and trains

Tips & Tricks:

  • Time your jumps carefully
  • Use power-ups strategically
  • Try to complete missions for rewards
  • Collect coins to upgrade power-ups
